Occupational Health service
for your employees, trust the NHS.

Based in Bristol, our team of Occupational Health specialists is one of the largest in the country. We offer a full range of services, designed to help people be healthy at work, and to help workplaces be healthy for people.

Following the pandemic, we now offer more of our services virtually. We also work on site and hold appointments at clinics in Whitefriars, xx and xx in Bristol.

We can tailor a package of services that will meet the needs of your teams.

  • Our support for employees includes physiotherapy, vaccinations, counselling, and planning a return to work after extended periods of sickness absence.
  • Our support for managers includes help managing people with long-term sickness absence, and making ‘reasonable adjustments’ for people in at risk groups.
  • Our support for employers includes assessing and monitoring work environments (‘health surveillance’ and ‘health screening’) and carrying out pre-employment health checks.

We work hard to maintain the high clinical standards our clients expect. We have SEQWAS and ISO 9000 accreditation, and are active members of ‘Growing Occupational Health’, the NHS-led forum driving innovation in Occupational Health services.
