A physiotherapist can help with you quickly recover from, and manage the symptoms of musculoskeletal issues, including:

  • Joint, muscle and spinal problems
  • New or existing conditions, for example, back pain or sports injuries
What you need to know
  • Check you are eligible for this service by asking your employer, or calling our helpline.
  • Physio Direct is not an emergency service. If you have sustained a serious injury or suspect a broken bone you should visit your nearest A&E department. If you require medication you should contact your GP.
How does the service work?
  • You can refer yourself (this doesn’t need to be booked through your manager).
  • Your first appointment is likely to be a telephone consultation, followed by face-to-face physiotherapy as required.
  • Book your appointment by calling our helpline. A Senior Chartered HCPC registered physiotherapist will then contact you during the pre-arranged time slot.
